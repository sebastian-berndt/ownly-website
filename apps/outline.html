About Outline

Outline is a modern, fast team wiki and knowledge base designed to be the single source of truth for your organization. Its intuitive interface makes it easy for anyone on your team to create, organize, and find the information they need.

With its focus on speed and collaboration, Outline helps teams document processes, onboard new members, and share knowledge effectively. The markdown-based editor ensures content is portable and future-proof.

  • Blazing fast markdown editor
  • Real-time multiplayer editing
  • Full-text search & filters
  • Document version history
  • Slack & Zapier integrations
  • Public document sharing
