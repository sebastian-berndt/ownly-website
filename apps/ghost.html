About Ghost

Ghost is a powerful, open-source publishing platform designed for creators who want to build a sustainable business around their content. It provides everything you need to publish, share, and grow a membership business around your content.

With native support for memberships, newsletters, and paid subscriptions, Ghost eliminates the need for multiple tools and third-party services, giving you a single platform to manage your entire content business.

  • Rich content editor with cards
  • Built-in membership & payments
  • Native email newsletters
  • SEO optimized out of the box
  • Headless CMS with Content API
  • Custom themes & integrations
