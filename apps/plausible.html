About Plausible

Plausible is a lightweight, open-source web analytics tool that provides all the essential website traffic insights without compromising visitor privacy. It's designed as a simple, transparent alternative to Google Analytics that respects user privacy.

With Plausible, you get a clean, intuitive dashboard that shows you the most important metrics at a glance. No complex setup, no training required—just actionable data about your website's performance.

  • No cookies or personal data
  • GDPR, CCPA & PECR compliant
  • Under 1KB tracking script
  • Custom event & goal tracking
  • Email & Slack reports
  • Stats API for integrations
