About WordPress

WordPress is the world's most popular content management system, powering over 40% of all websites on the internet. Its flexibility, extensive plugin ecosystem, and active community make it suitable for everything from simple blogs to complex enterprise websites.

Self-hosting WordPress gives you complete control over your site, your data, and your customization options—without the limitations of managed WordPress hosting platforms.

  • 59,000+ free plugins
