Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the product and billing.

Do I pay for my servers through Ownly?

No. Ownly is strictly a management platform. You provide an API key for your preferred host (like Hetzner, DigitalOcean, or AWS), and they will bill you directly for the server resources you consume.

What happens if I cancel my Ownly subscription?

Your servers keep running! Because Ownly uses standard Docker containers on your own infrastructure, all your apps will continue to function normally. You simply lose access to the Ownly dashboard for easy management and updates.

Are there any hidden fees for Marketplace apps?

Most open-source applications in our Marketplace are completely free. If an application requires a commercial license from the developer, we clearly mark it, but Ownly does not charge any premium or markup on apps.

Can I upgrade from Hobbyist to Professional later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. The transition is seamless and your connected servers or deployed applications will not experience any downtime during the billing change.

How do automated backups work?

On Professional and Business plans, you can connect any S3-compatible storage bucket (like AWS S3, Cloudflare R2, or Backblaze B2). Ownly will orchestrate encrypted database dumps and file volume backups to your bucket automatically.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 14-day free trial on our paid plans so you can thoroughly test the platform. If you're charged after the trial and are unsatisfied, we provide a full refund within the first 7 days of your first billing cycle.
