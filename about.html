Empowering Digital Autonomy

We believe everyone should have control over their own data and software. Ownly is building the bridge between complex infrastructure and everyday users.

Our Story

Reclaiming the internet, one server at a time.

In a world dominated by a few massive tech giants, our personal data is no longer our own. We realized that while open-source software offers a way out, the technical barrier to entry for self-hosting is simply too high for most people.

We built Ownly to democratize server management. By combining one-click app installations with the freedom to choose your own infrastructure provider, we're making digital independence accessible to everyone, regardless of their technical background.

Our Core Values

Everything we build is guided by a commitment to user freedom and simplicity.

True Ownership

Your data, your servers. We don't lock you in. You have root access to everything you build.

Privacy First

We collect zero telemetry from your instances. What happens on your server stays on your server.

Radical Simplicity

Self-hosting shouldn't require a computer science degree. We make complex tasks as easy as clicking a button.

Open Ecosystem

We strictly use and contribute back to open-source software, supporting the developers who build the tools we love.
