Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before signing up.

Do I need any technical knowledge to use Ownly?

No. That's exactly the problem we're solving. Ownly handles all the server configuration, software installation, and network routing automatically. If you can click "Install" on an app store, you can run your own private server.

Who manages the server?

You rent the server directly from a hosting provider, so you own it and pay them directly. Ownly provides the dashboard and tooling to set it up and install apps on it without any technical knowledge. We never have root access to your server.

Which apps will be available at launch?

We're launching with the most popular self-hostable apps: Nextcloud (file storage & collaboration), Bitwarden / Vaultwarden (password manager), Jellyfin (media server), Ghost (publishing), Immich (photo backup), and more. The marketplace will grow continuously.

How is Ownly different from just renting a VPS?

A raw VPS requires SSH access, Linux knowledge, Docker setup, SSL certificate configuration, firewall rules, and ongoing maintenance. Ownly gives you all of that through a simple UI. No terminal, no config files, no headaches. Think of it as the app store for your own server.

Can Ownly see or access my data?

No. Your data lives entirely on your server, not ours. Ownly never stores, scans, or has access to your files, passwords, or photos. We only communicate with your server to deploy and update the software. Privacy is guaranteed by our architecture.

What if I want to stop using Ownly?

Because the apps run on your own server, you're never locked in. Cancel Ownly and your apps keep running. They're standard open-source software. Your data stays exactly where it is. No export needed, no hostage data.

How much does it cost?

Pricing details will be announced at launch. Our goal is a flat monthly fee for Ownly, regardless of how many apps you run. You also pay your hosting provider separately for the server (typically €3–10/month for a small VPS). Early access members will receive a founding discount.
