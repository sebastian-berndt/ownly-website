Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Ownly › Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Ownly ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website or sign up for our waitlist.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate the waitlist:

  • Email address — provided voluntarily when you sign up for early access.
  • Timestamp — the date and time your email was submitted.

We do not collect your name, phone number, payment information, or any other personal data at this stage.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your email address is used solely to:

  • Notify you when Ownly launches or early access becomes available.
  • Send relevant product updates you signed up to receive.

We will never sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Waitlist emails are stored in Supabase, a hosted PostgreSQL database service. Data is stored on servers located within the European Union. Supabase's own privacy policy applies to data processed on their infrastructure.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We currently do not use tracking cookies, analytics tools, or third-party advertising scripts on this website. Any future addition of analytics will be disclosed in an updated version of this policy.

5. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection law (including the GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address listed in the Imprint.

6. Data Retention

We retain waitlist email addresses until the product launches and you have been notified, or until you request deletion. After the launch period, emails not converted to active accounts will be deleted within 90 days.

7. Third-Party Services

This website loads the Supabase JavaScript client from a CDN (cdn.jsdelivr.net). No other third-party scripts are loaded at this time.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be noted with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

9. Contact

For privacy-related enquiries, see our Imprint for contact details.
