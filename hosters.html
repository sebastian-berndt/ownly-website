Why It Matters

The CLOUD Act problem

The US CLOUD Act gives US authorities access to data stored by US companies — regardless of where the server is physically located. If your data is on AWS, Google Cloud, or Azure, it's subject to US law even if the server is in Frankfurt.

By choosing an EU Sovereign provider on Ownly, you get a genuine guarantee: EU company, EU data center, EU law. No US company in the chain means no CLOUD Act exposure.
